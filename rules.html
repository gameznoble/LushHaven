Rules & Punishments
Tier 1: Mild Punishments
Warn → 15m Mute → 30m Mute → 1h Mute → 3h Mute → 12h Mute

Spamming: Character, Similar, Unique, or Command spam (3+ times).

Foreign Languages: English only in main chat. (Punishments are Bans).

Politics/Religion: Disallowed to prevent drama.

Impersonation: Faking identity as staff or players.

Tier 3: Serious Punishments
1d Ban → 3d Ban → 7d Ban → 14d Ban → Permanent Ban

In-Game Scamming: Failure to deliver trades or misleading /ah items.

Griefing: Malicious destruction of claims or trusted areas.

TP Trapping: Killing players via warps or portal traps.

Blacklist Offenses

Non-appealable for 90 days. Reserved for Doxing, DDOS threats, sharing personal info, or extreme bug abuse.
