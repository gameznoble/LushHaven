Server Regulations

Last Updated: April 2026 • All players are expected to follow these guidelines.

Tier 1 Mild Punishments
Punishment Ladder
Warn ➔ 15m Mute ➔ 30m Mute ➔ 1h Mute ➔ 3h Mute ➔ 12h Mute

Applies to public chat unless otherwise reported.

Spamming

Character Spam, Similar Message Spam (3+), Unique Message Spam, or Command Spam (Bounties, /afk, etc).

Communication

Foreign Languages: English only. Discussion: No Politics or Religion. Swearing: Indirect swearing only (WTF is okay).

Impersonation

Claiming to be a player, staff, dev, or ex-staff member through nicknames or alts.

Tier 2 Moderate Punishments
Punishment Ladder
Warn ➔ 30m Mute ➔ 1h Mute ➔ 3h Mute ➔ 12h Mute ➔ 1d Mute

Inappropriate Conduct

Sexual, vulgar, or drug-related topics (UK Laws). Includes skins, capes, builds, or /ah items.

Targeting Staff

Maliciously attempting to expose staff in /vanish to cause disruption.

Trading & Economy

Disallowed Trading: IRL trades or 'Lemon Loans' over 10. (Bans replace mutes for this offense).

Tier 3 Serious Punishments
Punishment Ladder
1d Ban ➔ 3d Ban ➔ 7d Ban ➔ 14d Ban ➔ Permanent Ban

Harassment

Bullying, drama, misinformation, or negative connotations about players.

Griefing & Trapping

TP/Portal trapping or destroying/stealing in claims (Survival) or islands (Skyblock).

Scamming

Failure to deliver trade deals or misleading auction house listings.

Dangerous Conduct

IRL threats, encouraging self-harm, or non-malicious bug/mod abuse.

Tier 4 Severe Punishments
Punishment Ladder
7d Ban ➔ 14d Ban ➔ 30d Ban ➔ Permanent Ban

Discrimination

Marginalization based on identity, race, or sexuality. Includes all slurs.

Malicious Bug/Mod Abuse

Significant abuse impacting economy, server performance, or using Hacked Clients.

Extra Exceptions & Special Cases

Advertising

Non-malicious (IP sharing) = 6h Mute to P-Mute. Malicious = Permanent Mute.

Toxicity/Nuisance

Direct server disrespect or being a general nuisance (1d Mute to P-Mute).

Alt Limits

Bypassing 5-account limit via VPN (30d to P-Ban).
