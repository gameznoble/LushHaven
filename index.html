LushHaven Code of Conduct

Effective: April 17, 2026 • Version 4.1

By joining LushHaven Network, you agree to follow all rules below. Ignorance is not an excuse.

💬 Chat & Community Rules

  • English Only: Public chat must be in English for clarity.
  • Respectful Language: No excessive toxicity, hate speech, or harassment.
  • Advertising: No promoting other servers or external links.
  • Scamming (ZERO TOLERANCE): Any scam = severe punishment including bans.
  • Evidence Based Action: Staff may restore items if clear proof is provided.

🎮 Gameplay Rules

  • Cheating: Hacked clients, macros, or unfair mods are not allowed.
  • Exploiting: Bug abuse is strictly forbidden and must be reported.
  • Account Responsibility: You are responsible for your account actions.

💰 Economy & Fair Play

  • Duping: Any duplication exploit is permanent ban level offense.
  • Trading: All trades are final unless proven scam.
  • Economy Abuse: Exploiting systems is strictly forbidden.

⚠️ Punishment System

Minor Offenses

Warnings • Temporary Mutes • Short Kicks

Moderate Offenses

Temporary Ban • Economy Reset • Item Removal

Severe Offenses

Permanent Ban • Blacklist • IP Ban (if needed)

Staff decisions are final. These rules exist to protect fairness and community integrity.

By playing on LushHaven, you agree to follow all rules above.
